I downloaded Pseudomonas savastanoi pv. phaseolicola 1448A data for this assignment. I downloaded 3 WT RNAseq data as the control and 3 mutant RNAseq data as the treatment.
All samples showed good overall sequence quality, with read lengths of 150 bp and consistent GC content (52%). Most quality metrics passed, though a few R2 reads showed warnings for adapter content, indicating minor residual adapter sequences.
The PCA plot shows: PC1 explained about 70.4% of the variation in the data, while PC2 explained 14.5%, showing that most differences between samples are captured by PC1. The control samples clustered closely together, indicating similar gene expression among replicates. In contrast, the treatment samples were more spread out, suggesting greater variability in gene expression after treatment.
The Heatmap correlation f shows high correlations among all samples, indicating overall similar gene expression profiles and good data quality. Control samples show strong similarity with each other, while treatment samples display slightly more variability, which seems consistent with the greater spread observed in the PCA plot.
